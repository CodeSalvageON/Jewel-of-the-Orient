Many years ago, merchants, travelers, and pilgrims alike relied on the famed Eastern Silk Road in order to get to their destinations. In this simulation, you will embark on a long and ardous quest, taking the role of an up-and-coming merchant attempting to turn a profit. However, starvation, robbers, and disease will challenge you at every turn. How will you fare?

You will travel hundreds of miles from city to city, fending off bandits, bad weather, diseases, and even starvation, if you aren't careful.

You have a limited amount of arrows and the trail is harsh. Good luck!
